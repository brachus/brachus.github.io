RoomExplorer.js

A quick-n-dirty 2-d dungeon crawler where I have future plans of developing it into a game. It's nothing too extravagant, but I'm trying to at least create a solid player experience. On the technical side, I'm pretty sure there are many solutions, like the way scripts are made for it, where better solutions might have been implemented.
The main point was to get something out there that could offer a decent player experience.
